Research

I'm interested in developing solutions that enhance the reliability and safety of deep learning models for real-world deployment. In general, I am motivated by the application of deep learning in safety critical applications such as healthcare. Recently, I have developed a keen interest in the safety and alignment aspects of foundation models.
